Biography

I am a PhD student at Department of Geomatics Engineering, University of Calgary.

Before coming to U of Calgary, I obtained a master's degree in Cartography and Geographic Information System from Institute of Remote Sensing and Digital Earth, Chinese Academy of Sciences (Beijing, China) in 2016. I got my bachelor degree in Geodesy and Geomatics from School of Geodesy and Geomatics, Wuhan University (Wuhan, China) in 2013.

My research interests lie in Mobile mapping, Vegetation mapping and Remote sensing.
